Marching Spotlight!

One way to get better at Marching Band is by watching other Marching Bands that possess the "next-level" skills that we are trying to obtain! Marching Spotlight features various spectacular High School Marching Bands, College Bands, DCI groups, and possibly even WGI performances!

 

Year: 2026

Show Name: The End.

Group: Rhythm X

Location: WGI World Championships

Level: WGI PIW

Context: Rhythm X's 2026 production "The End.", was a cinematic portrayal of finality, closure, and what comes after collapse. The show used a minimalist yet heavy atmosphere alongside stark visuals, fragmented musical statements, and moments of silence and release to construct a feeling of inevitability. Take an emotional journey through Rhythm X's story as they earned the WGI PIW Championship and the highest score in PIW history: 99.300.
